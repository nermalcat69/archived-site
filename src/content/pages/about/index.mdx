### My Personality Traits

I love it simple and I love to be very straightforward - everything can be simplified.

> "It's so simple to be happy but so difficult to be simple" - Rabindranath Tagore

Materialism is a mere part of my life, Which helps me to connect with people who value it over thoughts and I'm lucky to have these resources
as it opens so many doors.

I like optimizating and oversimplifying things to people to the point where anyone can understand it, Sometimes people find me weird as if i'm high on drugs.

The most difficult part for me is selling myself than my own product, specifically when it comes to interviews where i'm
supposed to explain my self *there's no self*. This is one of the reasons why I started this website so that I can express
myself as Selling products is comparatively easier than explaining the self.
